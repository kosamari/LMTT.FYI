Privacy Policy

Hey, you're probably wondering what's going to happen to your data should you read or write a post on lmtt.fyi. Here is a list of things we do with your data.

What data do we collect ?

If you are just reading posts on lmtt.fyi, we DO NOT collect any personal data.

When you post something on lmtt.fyi, here are list of information we collect (we use Twitter OAuth).

  • Your Twitter user name
  • Your Twitter display name
  • Your Twitter profile image
  • Contents you written on lmtt.fyi
We do not collect your email address or password, and are not able to post anything to your Twitter account.

How will we use your data ?

We will NOT ...

  • sell or give away your data to third parties
  • contact you without your permission
  • use your data for any reason other than to assist with making lmtt.fyi a better experience

We might ...

  • use your data to perform analysis on site usage
  • publish data visualization of the site usage analysis

Every post on lmtt.fyi is shared as public post. Anyone who has link to your post can see what you wrote. Please see Content Policy for more information.
