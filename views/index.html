Welcome to LMTT.FYI

Hi there, so, I've been seeing a lot of tweets with screen capture of another tweet or news site. While image can fit a lot of information, reading text in picture is hard for me as ESL. In many case, it is not accessible to screen reader either.


Twitter offers user to put image description, but I often forget, and I can't add image description after I tweeted.


So I had the idea of a mini app to add image description, translation, and commentary to a tweet, without character limit & edit capability.

How to write post

Step 1
Go to individual tweet page, add lmtt.fyi/ in front of the url.





Step 2
Click on CREATE NEW POST button, write comments and hit save ! You will need to authenticate with your Twitter account if you are not logged in yet.
